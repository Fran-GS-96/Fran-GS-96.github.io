About Me

Who Am I?

I am a physicist and PhD candidate at the Universidad de Chile with a diverse research background. My academic journey has allowed me to study complex systems ranging from the scales of Gravity and Cosmology to Meteorology and Climatology.

Currently, my PhD research is focused on Active Matter, where I explore the dynamics of self-propelled particles and statistical mechanics. Beyond my research, I have experience as a consultant and project engineer, and I am currently leading my own startup, Manqay, to develop computer vision solutions.
