About Me

Who Am I?

I am a PhD student at the Universidad de Chile with a diverse research background. My academic journey has allowed me to study complex systems ranging from the scales of Gravity and Cosmology to Meteorology and Climatology.

Currently, my PhD research is focused on Active Matter, where I explore the dynamics of self-propelled particles and statistical mechanics.

Beyond my research, I have experience as a Consultant and as an Entrepeneur.

An important part of my life is doing sports. I practiced Taekwondo and Basketball during my childhood and early in my adult life and nowadays I have develop the routine to go to the GYM weekly and I am currently preparing to run my first Marathon.
